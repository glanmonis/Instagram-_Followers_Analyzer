      How to — Export Instagram Data

      Getting your files

      01

      Open the Instagram app or website and go to Settings.

      02

      Navigate to Accounts Center → Your Information and Permissions → Download Your Information.

      03

      Select your account, choose JSON as the format, then request the download.

      04

      Wait for an email, download and extract the ZIP file, then upload followers.json and following.json above.
